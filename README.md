Food Court Management System

The Food Court Management System is a software application designed to streamline and automate daily operations in a food court environment. The system manages food orders, menu items, billing, and customer records, enabling efficient coordination between customers, vendors, and administrators.

By digitizing manual processes, the system improves order accuracy, reduces waiting time, and enhances overall service efficiency.

Key Features

Menu management and updates
Order placement and tracking
Billing and payment management
Order history and reporting
Admin and vendor management
